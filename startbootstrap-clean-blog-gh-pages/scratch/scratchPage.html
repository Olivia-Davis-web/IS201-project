Indoor Rock Climbing

Everything you need to know for your first time!

Indoor rock climbing can be a fun and safe way to harness your sense of adventure (pun intented). To get started there are a few things that you should know so that you can stay safe and ensure a good time.


Gear

  1. Harness

  2. Belay Device

    There are many different types but the three most common are:

    • Grigri
    • ATC Pilot
    • ATC XP


  3. Lead climbing clips


  4. Chalk


  5. Climbing Shoes


Types of Climbing

  1. Top Rope

    2. Top rope climbing is a type of rock climbing where a climber is attached to a rope that runs from their harness to an anchor at the top of the climb and back down to a belayer on the ground.

    For Top Rope climbing you will need:

    • Harness
    • Climbing shoes
    • Chalk
    • A parter to belay you using a belay device


  2. Lead Climbing

    3. Lead climbing is a rock climbing technique where the climber clips their rope to protection points on the wall as they ascend. Gyms that have lead climbing walls have set places to clip into as you climb.

    For lead climbing you will need:

    • Harness
    • Climbing shoes
    • Climbing clips
    • Chalk
    • A partner to belay you using a belay device

  3. Bouldering

    4. Bouldering is on a shorter wall where you test your climbing skills without a harness or rope. Because there is thick comfortable padding at the bottom of short bouldering walls, you don't have to worry about getting hurt if you fall.

    For bouldering you will need:

    • Climbing shoes
    • Chalk


Climbing Tips

  1. Make sure you have all your gear secured.

  2. Start at smaller levels and then move up.

  3. Use your feet to support you, don't try to reach too high with your arms.
